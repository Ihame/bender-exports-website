About Us

The Best Choice For your Business with over 6 Years of experience

Bender Export Ltd was founded in 2017 and is registered in Rwanda under the RDB Company code of 107432539. In addition to producing, processing and exporting coffee, we also offer services for renting construction & heavy equipment.
